--- layout: post title: Visual Undergrounding of Electrical Wires of Noe Valley date: '2013-04-12T17:01:00.001-07:00' author: ndp tags: - analysis - design - prototyping - mini-project - demo - jQuery - visualization modified_time: '2013-04-12T17:01:26.337-07:00' thumbnail: http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-WR6d3B9dxoE/UWieohiDU7I/AAAAAAAAB8M/EQiTar_bPH8/s72-c/Screen+Shot+2013-04-12+at+4.53.23+PM.png --- All the visual clutter of the city gets to me. There are wires and (often confusing) signs everywhere. I imagine sometime if all this noise could just be silenced. I took an hour and some crude Photoshop skills to envision parts of my neighborhood Noe Valley, San Francisco without all the wires.

These are presented using a NYTimes technique for before/after photos. Go to the page, and then drag the black dividing line to compare the photos.






As part of a 100-year plan, PG&E is undergrounding some of these wires, but Muni wires will be with us for a long time.
